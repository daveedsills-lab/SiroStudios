How can we make more on OnlyFans?

1. Create High-Quality Content

High-quality content is a prerequisite to going viral on any social media platform, from Instagram to YouTube to Twitter, to Reddit and OnlyFans. There is no real and deep explanation for why the content quality matters so drastically; it’s just the human condition that appeals to more precise and more defined things. More so, it does leave a massive impression on your audience base; it lets them understand that you, as a content creator, prioritize and place quality on your work, which makes them respect and fall in love deeper with you.

2. Being in a Blue Ocean Niche

Every creator on these social platforms is going in the same direction regarding marketing themselves; they position themselves as “the classic only girl.” That is the most generic way to go about it; the creators in the top 1% and higher have something utterly unique about their brand, their content, and how they portray themselves to the outside world. They place their individuality and personality behind their content and brand, which gives a seal of authenticity that is easy to spot for fans and makes them feel more trusted as influencers.

‍3. Consistency

This is one of the more basic tenets for generating more revenue. It’s a simple common law of the universe that if a certain audience sees your content repeatedly, your brand is lasered in their brain, and whenever they see your content again, they feel familiar, which plays a massive role in the long game of really turning subscribers into whale fans.

Especially with all social media platforms,  their algorithms push your videos and help you get more reach if you are consistent with posting. These platforms are just that, platforms. They aim to have as many users as possible on their app and spend the most time possible. If you post daily, you are deliberately forcing your way into the algorithm to push your video out. When finding the right vertical, consistency must be an underlying factor, as that allows you to properly A/B split test what works and what doesn't along a constant time frame.

5. Offer an Exclusive

With OnlyFans, the game is about exclusivity and how you present that to your fans. There are many ways to go about this; some creators have a free page and then a VIP page, which is exclusive. Some creators would offer special requests such as Facetime or phone calls. Other creators may create different tiers inside their VIP page to give levels to what type of fans can get what kind of content. The more special content goes to the fans at the higher tiers. The whole point of an exclusive is to offer an intimate experience; luckily for you, the fan already sees subscribing to your page as the beginning of that experience, a foot in the door. Adding more clever levels that fans want to see will provide you with a resource to generate more revenue while keeping a perfect brand image!

6. Collaborate with other Creators

Collaborating with other creators is a game that can benefit all parties involved, and it is a win-win for everyone. The big focus is the additional content that will be shared across multiple/numerous audience bases, but there are so many additional factors that make it all worth it. For example, you can understand how other content creators work, post, and interact with their fans; knowledge of operations from creators (significantly more prominent creators) is a cheat code for success as they give you the blueprint. Another significant factor is that you can merge your audience base, meaning that you can have a significant amount of an entire massive creator’s fan base eye on your content and page on OnlyFans. This works exceptionally well when you collaborate with someone who visually may look like you or maybe inside the same niche or vertical in which you do content. This allows the same target market audience to align, making this process more profitable and significant.

‍7. Tailor Content to your fans

Many creators have one big issue when creating content: figuring out what to record. Most of them go down the path of subscribing to other creators to see what they’re doing regarding content on their wall, mass messages, PPV, etc. The most significant help that they can ever dream of is under their noses, their fans. Your audience is the best way to understand what content resonates with them. Understanding other factors, such as when most of your audience is awake, will help you find the best times to post your content.

8. Market Research

Within this industry, you do not need to reinvent the wheel. Hundreds of creators generate over six figures a month, and their strategy for getting there is easily found if you know what to look for. Reverse engineering creators’ social media accounts, such as their Instagram or Twitter feed, will allow you to see what the creator does daily and the unique little factors that help compound their brand to new heights. Visualizing these “flow charts” of traffic on a whiteboard software like Figma allows you to understand every factor of their strategy(s), especially once done across numerous creators.

Conclusion:

Being successful on OnlyFans boils down to the basics, Marketing and Account Management, & how well you can execute both operations. A bunch of foundational work needs to be implemented throughout your work. Remember, there is no light without rain, so there will be periods where you fail, and you will genuinely blossom with success once you overcome those periods of failure by being you. Being the genuine version of you, combined with the basics of consistency, quality, creativity, and other factors, will allow you to shine and your revenue to reflect that.
